What is alternative medicine?



Alternative medicine is a broad term that encompasses various health practices and treatments that fall outside of conventional Western medicine. These practices are often rooted in traditional, cultural, or holistic approaches to health and wellness.

What are some examples of alternative medicine practices?



Alternative medicine practices include acupuncture, herbal remedies, chiropractic care, massage therapy, meditation, yoga, and many others.
